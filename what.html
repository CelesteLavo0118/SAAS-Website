What we do

StudioRoom provides you with multiple key features such as:

  1. Customize your creative team
  2. Manage your projects and media

StudioRoom reaches out to designers eager to put together the perfect creative team suitable for specific projects. This tool targets the whole creative team: from web developers, to art directors, to photographers and more. StudioRoom invites designers looking to bring projects to life with local users.
